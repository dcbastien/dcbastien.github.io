Intro

Hi, I'm DC.
Nice to meet you.

I am an aspiring web developer, biomedical researcher, energetic twin mom, and avid college football wife!

The COVID-19 pandemic inspired me to broaden my knowledge base and begin a journey to learn more about web development.

I worked as a biomedical research scientist for almost 5 years and I am excited to merge my expertise in science and technology. Throughout the COVID pandemic there has been an abundance of misinformation shared amongst the general population about COVID-19, risk factors, and its specific impact on people of color. This gap inspired me to take a look at ways to help.

My goal is to help fellow professionals create stunning displays of data, that are easy to understand for all audiences. I also believe professionals can benefit from bridging the divide between academia and the general population. Connecting to audiences through the fast paced and ever evolving tech world can be easier by providing visibility through social media and professional web design.

Check out a few of my recent projects or view my resume .
